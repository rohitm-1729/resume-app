About DataFlow Inc

DataFlow Inc is the leading real-time analytics platform trusted by 500+ enterprises to process and visualize billions of events per day. Our data infrastructure team builds the backbone that powers our customers' insights.

About the Role

We are looking for a Staff Software Engineer to join our Data Infrastructure team. You will be responsible for designing, building, and scaling our core data pipelines and streaming systems. You will also serve as a technical lead, mentoring engineers and influencing architectural decisions across the organization.

Responsibilities

  • Design and build high-throughput, fault-tolerant data pipelines that process millions of events per second
  • Lead technical design reviews and architecture discussions across teams
  • Mentor and grow a team of 4-6 engineers through code reviews, 1:1s, and structured development plans
  • Partner with product managers and data scientists to translate requirements into robust technical solutions
  • Drive adoption of best practices in observability, testing, and infrastructure-as-code
  • Own reliability and performance SLAs for critical data infrastructure components

Requirements

  • 7+ years of software engineering experience with 2+ years in a senior or staff-level role
  • Deep expertise in stream processing frameworks (Kafka, Flink, Spark Streaming, or similar)
  • Strong proficiency in Python, Go, or Java for backend systems
  • Experience building and operating distributed systems at scale
  • Proven track record of technical leadership and cross-functional collaboration
  • Strong SQL skills and experience with columnar or OLAP databases

Nice to Have

  • Experience with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift)
  • Contributions to open-source data tools
  • Experience in a high-growth startup environment
  • Knowledge of infrastructure-as-code tools (Terraform, Pulumi)
